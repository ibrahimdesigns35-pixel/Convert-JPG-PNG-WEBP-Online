Privacy Policy

1. Commitment to Privacy

At Free Image Converter Tool, your privacy is our top priority. Unlike other online converters, we do not upload your images to our servers. All image processing is handled locally within your web browser.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data or images you process through our tool. Our website is designed to be fully functional without storing any of your files on a remote server.

3. Cookies

We may use minimal cookies to improve your user experience, such as remembering your preferred conversion format. These cookies do not store personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use CDNs (like Google Fonts and Font Awesome) to deliver design assets. These providers may collect your IP address as part of the asset delivery process, but this is standard across the web.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, feel free to contact us at support@freeimageconvertertool.com.
